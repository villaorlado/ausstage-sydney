Chao1 for incomplete performance archives

This toy model treats each performance as an event containing a set of actors. In the observed archive, some performances may be missing entirely, and some recorded performances may omit actors. From the observed time slice, we count actors who appear once (singletons, f₁) and twice (doubletons, f₂) to estimate unseen actors with Chao1.
